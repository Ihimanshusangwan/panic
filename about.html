Welcome to Your Panic Button Site

Are you looking for peace of mind and security at your fingertips?

Our Panic Button is here to ensure your safety in critical situations.

Key Features:

  • Instant Assistance: With just a single tap, reach out to emergency contacts, local authorities, or our 24/7 support team.
  • Real-time Location Sharing: Let your trusted contacts track your location in real-time during emergencies.

How It Works:

  • Simple Setup: Signup , create your profile, and set up your emergency contacts.
  • Emergency Activation: In a threatening situation, tap the panic button or use the predefined gesture to send out an alert.
  • Assistance Arrives: Emergency responders or your selected contacts receive your location and details for prompt assistance.

Who Should Use This App:

  • - Individuals living alone
  • - Students walking home late
  • - Travelers exploring new places
  • - Seniors who need immediate help
  • - Employees working late hours
